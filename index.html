I am a junior at DePaul University studying computer science, graphic design, and Mandarin. As someone who’s very interested in both coding and design, it seems to me many graphic designers are (naturally) unaware of what goes on behind the scenes during the development of software and web applications, and many computer scientists don’t know how to interact with the user in the most aesthetically pleasing, user-friendly manner. My goal is to become proficient enough in both fields so that as I continue to work on web and software development, I can help better bridge the gap between the two.

I have also been working on a full stack development certification through Free Code Camp, and I have been furthering my Python coding skills through HackerRank. This quarter at DePaul I’m enrolled in Object-Oriented Software Development, Foundations of Artificial Intelligence, and Mobile App Development for iOS.

I am a cofounder of Higher Ground Apparel, a clothing company focused on further exploring your immediate surroundings instead of wishing to be elsewhere. It has been a company for two and a half years and has served as a fantastic creative outlet for me. Through Higher Ground, I have been able to immensely practice web and graphic design, along with learn how to work with other companies and people to produce a desired idea and product.

Originally from Columbus, Ohio, I am currently living in Chicago, Illinois. Among many things, I love tennis, drinking water, climbing, fashion, and experiencing other cultures. I studied Mandarin in Shanghai, China last summer and I recently traveled to Israel for my Birthright trip. Every time I go to a foreign land and indulge in different cultures I learn a great deal about myself, and I hope I can continue to travel to new places, meet new people, and learn new languages.
